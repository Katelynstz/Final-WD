Michelle Lewis

Michelle is a newborn photographer who has a natural talent for capturing the innocence and sweetness of newborn babies. With a gentle touch and a calm demeanor, she ensures that both the baby and parents feel comfortable throughout the session. Michelle's pricing for newborn sessions starts at $250, including a variety of props and outfits, edited digital images, and a personalized slideshow. Her ability to capture the fleeting moments of a baby's early days makes her a popular choice for new parents.
