Jessica Bennett

Jessica is a family photographer who believes in capturing genuine moments that reflect the love and connection within families. With a friendly and relaxed approach, she creates a comfortable environment for families to express themselves authentically. Jessica's pricing starts at $200 for a one-hour outdoor session, including edited digital images and a private online gallery. Her ability to capture the unique dynamics of each family makes her a trusted choice for those seeking heartfelt memories.
